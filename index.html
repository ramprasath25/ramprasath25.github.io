Education and early life[edit]

I was born in Tiruchirappalli, Tamilnadu, India,[3] to Rajagopalan and shyamala in Tamil speaking family. I grew up and spent my childhood in Madurai, Tamilnadu, India.

I completed my schooling in TVS Higher Seconday school, Madurai in 2008. I earned my Bachelor of Engineering on Electronics and Communication from Sri Vidya College of Engineering and Technology, Virudhunagar, Tamilnadu, India (Anna University) in 2012.

Career[edit]

After completion of degree, I moved to Bangalore, Karnataka, India in search of job. I started learning android and got interested in learning web technologies and applications.

I started my carrer at Stratvave Innovations which is a product based startup doing web application. There i worked as software developer in developing web based applications.

Later i moved to mSupply.com ecommerce pvt ltd., as senior software developer. My responsiblity was to deliver the RESTApi for the webapplication and mobile application using Node js technology.

Currently i work for Alten calsoft labs which i am deployed to client Colortokens Inc., Bengaluru which is cyber secured product based startup headquartered Santa Clara, USA. My key role is to develop backend service as part of backend team.
